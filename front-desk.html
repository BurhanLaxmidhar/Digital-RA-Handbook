Front Desk & Move-In/Out

Essential guides and checklists for your busiest shifts as an RA.

Front Desk Duties

  • Check in new residents (ID verification through Purdue ID, key handout)
  • Answer resident and parent questions
  • Direct traffic to rooms or elevators
  • Hand out mail/packages
  • Loan out spare keys (log and follow policy!)
  • Monitor lobby area for safety & policy violations
  • Check out residents (collect key, complete paperwork)
  • Refer issues to professional staff when needed

Move-In Procedures

  1. Arrive early for your shift and get desk set up
  2. Check in with supervisor or hall director
  3. Welcome new residents and families
  4. Direct residents to their assigned rooms and answer questions
  5. Hand out keys and ensure that they have checked in on the housing portal
  6. Log any facility issues (damaged rooms, missing keys, etc.)
  7. Stay visible and approachable—smile!

Move-Out Procedures

  1. Inspect rooms for cleanliness and damages (use check-out procedures)
  2. Ensure all appliances are unplugged
  3. Set thermostat/AC to 75°F and close all windows
  4. Ensure all returned keys are correct
  5. Log damages or missing items for facilities
  6. Make a final round to ensure only approved late-stays remain

Pro Tips

  • Keep a list of important phone numbers at the desk (maintenance, police, pro staff)
  • Always document incidents or facility issues right away
  • Be calm and friendly—even during rush times
  • If you’re unsure, ask your REC/REA or a returning RA
