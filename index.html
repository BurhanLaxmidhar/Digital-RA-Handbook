What is This?

The Digital RA Handbook is a practical, easy-to-navigate guide for Resident Assistants and staff at Purdue. Find clear instructions, pro tips, and essential links—built by an RA, for RAs.

What Does an RA Do?

  • Directs move-in traffic and answers resident questions
  • Works front desk: checks in/out residents, logs packages, loans keys
  • Performs room checks, health & safety rounds
  • Plans and hosts community events
  • Connects students to campus resources
  • Reports emergencies and facilities issues
